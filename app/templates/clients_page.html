FAQ

Common questions.

How long does a typical engagement take?

Most projects run 4–8 weeks depending on scope. We'll agree on a timeline upfront and check in regularly to make sure we're on track.

Do you charge for your services?

No. As a student organization, our consulting services are provided at no cost. We ask only for your time, access to relevant information, and honest feedback on our work.

What do you need from us to get started?

Just a brief conversation. We'll ask about your goals, current setup, and any specific pain points. From there we scope the project together.

What if we don't know where AI fits yet?

That's exactly where we can help. An AI readiness assessment is often the best first step — it gives you a clear map of opportunities before committing to anything.

Can you build software or tools for us?

Our primary focus is strategy and consulting, not development. We can design a solution and help you understand what to build — but we'll be upfront if implementation is outside our scope.
