Recruiting Process

Applications Open

Submit your resume and a short application telling us why you're interested in the organization. No prior experience required, just demonstrate your interest and your willingness to participate.

Callout #1

Our first official info session. We'll talk about the club and walk through what to expect in the recruitment cycle. We will also practice a case together as a group. This is your chance to get comfortable with what a case interview looks like. Come ready to engage and ask questions.

Applications Close

Make sure to submit your application by the deadline. After this point, we will review the candidates and invite the selected members for the next round.

Case Interview (by invitation)

A structured case interview similar to what you'd see in traditional consulting recruiting, and what we practiced in the callouts. Remember, we care more about how you think rather than getting a correct answer.

Coffee Chat

A very casual, no-pressure meet-and-greet with the founding members. Grab a coffee and a snack, ask questions about the club, and learn more about what we want to do. No preparation is needed!

Callout #2

Same content as the first callout. We just want to give you an option to choose a date and time that works best for you.

Behavioral Interview (by invitation)

In this interview, we are looking for enthusiasm and will ask you to share specific, examples from your past to learn about your background and motivation. Selected members will move on to the final round.

Final Decision!
